Certifications We Support

These certificates are required or recommended for Ethiopian marine engineers at different career stages.

STCW II/1 — COC

Certificate of Competency (Officer of the Watch)

The foundational certificate required to serve as a watchkeeping officer on any vessel globally.

Required
STCW III/1 — ENG OOW

Engineer Officer of the Watch

Required for all marine engineers to stand watches in the engine room on seagoing vessels.

Required
STCW VI/1 — BST

Basic Safety Training

Personal survival, fire prevention, first aid, and personal safety — mandatory for every seafarer.

Required
STCW III/2 — CHIEF ENG

Chief Engineer Certificate

Management-level certification for engineers aspiring to the highest engine department rank.

Advanced
STCW VI/3 — AFF

Advanced Fire Fighting

Required for officers designated to take charge of fire-fighting operations aboard ships.

Required for Officers
IGF CODE

Gas Fuelled Ships Certificate

Specialist certificate for engineers working on LNG/LPG carriers and gas-fuelled vessels.

Specialist
