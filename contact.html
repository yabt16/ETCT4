Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become an ECA member?+
Go to the Register page and fill in the membership form. Membership is free for cadets and maritime students. You will receive a confirmation email within 48 hours.
I finished my contract and I am home in Ethiopia. How do I find short-term work?+
Visit the Financial Support page and click on the "Between-Contract Jobs" tab. You will find vacancies posted by Ethiopian companies specifically for maritime professionals on their home leave period.
Are ECA courses internationally recognised?+
Yes. All STCW courses on our platform are delivered by certified instructors and lead to internationally recognised certificates. For non-STCW courses, certificates are issued by ECA and acknowledged by our partner institutions.
How do I apply for a scholarship?+
Scholarships are listed on the Financial Support page under the Scholarships tab. You must be a registered ECA member to apply. Each scholarship has its own requirements and deadlines listed on the card.
Can companies post job vacancies on ECA?+
Yes. Ethiopian companies can register on ECA as an employer and post short-term vacancies for maritime professionals on their home leave. Go to the Register page and select "Company / Employer" as your account type.
I am a senior marine engineer. How do I post a course?+
Register as an Instructor on the Register page. Our team will review your qualifications and experience, and if approved, you will receive access to the instructor dashboard where you can upload course videos and materials.
