Who We Are

A Community Built for Ethiopian Maritime Professionals

The Ethiopian Cadet Association (ECA) is a professional body dedicated to developing, supporting, and connecting Ethiopian marine engineering cadets and officers across the globe. We bridge the gap between training and career, providing resources, mentorship, and advocacy for our members.

From the shores of the Red Sea to oceans worldwide, our members serve aboard vessels of all kinds — and we are here to support every step of their journey.
